Who was Muhammed Ali

Muhammad Ali, original name Cassius Marcellus Clay.
American professional boxer and social activist.

  • Olympic gold medalist
  • World heavyweight boxing champion X3
  • 37 career Knockouts
  • Joined Nation of islam
  • Presidential Medal of Freedom

General Facts

  • Occupation : Boxer and Activist
  • Born : January 17, 1942
  • Place of Birth : Louisville,Kentucky, U.S
  • Died : June 3, 2016
  • Place Of Death : Scottsdale, Arizona
